Frequently Asked

How do I request a refund?
Go to Dashboard → Bookings → select your booking → click "Request Refund". Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.
How do I become a listed agency?
Register an account and select "I am a travel agency" during signup. After basic verification, your profile goes live within 24 hours.
Is my payment secure?
Yes. All payments are processed via Razorpay — a PCI DSS compliant payment gateway. We never store your card details.
How do I report a fraudulent agency?
Open a support ticket from your dashboard and select "Agency Complaint". Our team reviews all complaints within 2 hours and takes immediate action.
Can I cancel a booking?
Cancellation depends on the agency's policy. Contact the agency directly through the chat room, or raise a support ticket if they are unresponsive.
