The Process

How Trivasta works for agencies

1️⃣
Apply & get approved
Submit your agency details. Our team reviews applications within 24–48 hours. Once approved, you get instant access to your dashboard and can start listing packages immediately.
2️⃣
List your packages
Create detailed travel packages with descriptions, images, pricing and inclusions. Your packages appear on Trivasta's homepage and are shown to relevant travelers based on their trip type and destination.
3️⃣
Respond to trip requests
Travelers use our AI to plan trips and then look for agencies to execute them. You can see all live trip requests and send custom offers with your own pricing and message — creating a competitive marketplace that benefits travelers and agencies alike.
4️⃣
Receive secure payments
When a traveler books your offer, payment is processed securely via Razorpay. Trivasta collects a platform fee (5–10% depending on your plan) and the rest is settled directly to your account within 3–5 business days.
5️⃣
Build your reputation
After each trip, travelers rate your agency. Higher ratings mean better placement on the homepage, more visibility, and ultimately more bookings. Consistent quality is rewarded on Trivasta.

📋 Agency Terms & Conditions

Last updated: February 2026 · Please read carefully before registering

1. Eligibility & Registration

To register as a travel agency on Trivasta, you must be a legally registered travel business with a valid GST number (where applicable) operating in India. By registering, you confirm that your agency holds all necessary licenses and permits required under applicable Indian law to offer travel services.

  • You must provide accurate and complete information during registration
  • One agency account per registered business entity
  • Trivasta reserves the right to reject any application without giving reasons
  • Misrepresentation of agency information will result in immediate account termination
2. Subscription Plans & Billing

Agency access is offered through annual subscription plans (Starter, Professional, Enterprise). All plans are billed annually and include a 14-day free trial. No charges are applied during the trial period.

  • Subscriptions auto-renew annually unless cancelled 30 days before renewal
  • Annual fees are non-refundable after the 14-day trial period
  • Plan downgrades take effect at the next renewal date
  • Trivasta reserves the right to change pricing with 60 days' notice
3. Platform Commission

In addition to the annual subscription, Trivasta charges a platform fee on each completed booking. This fee varies by plan: 10% for Starter, 8% for Professional, and 5% for Enterprise. The platform fee is automatically deducted before payment settlement.

  • Fees are calculated on the total booking value including taxes
  • Payment settlements occur within 3–5 business days of trip confirmation
  • Disputed bookings are held until resolution
4. Package Listings & Content

Agencies are solely responsible for the accuracy and legality of all content they publish, including package descriptions, pricing, inclusions and images.

  • All pricing must be in Indian Rupees (INR) and inclusive of applicable taxes
  • Package images must be original or properly licensed — no copyright infringement
  • Misleading or false package descriptions are grounds for immediate suspension
  • Trivasta may remove any listing that violates these terms without notice
5. Conduct & Quality Standards
  • Agencies must honour confirmed bookings — cancellations harm traveler trust and your rating
  • Response to trip requests should be made within 48 hours
  • Agencies must maintain a minimum rating of 3.5★ to remain listed
  • Any attempt to contact travelers outside the Trivasta platform is prohibited
  • Offering kickbacks, fake reviews or manipulating ratings will result in permanent ban
6. Liability & Disputes

Trivasta acts solely as a marketplace connecting travelers with agencies. We are not responsible for the quality, safety or delivery of travel services. All disputes between agencies and travelers must be resolved between the parties, with Trivasta acting as a neutral mediator where necessary.

7. Account Termination

Trivasta reserves the right to suspend or terminate agency accounts for violations of these terms, repeated traveler complaints, fraudulent activity or breach of applicable law. Terminated agencies forfeit their annual subscription fee and any pending settlements may be withheld pending investigation.

8. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of India. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Mumbai, Maharashtra.
