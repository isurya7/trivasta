Our Journey

How Trivasta came to be

💡
The Idea
Frustrated travelers become builders
Surya and Ujan, two college friends with a shared love of travel, spent weeks trying to plan a Rajasthan trip — only to be overwhelmed by WhatsApp groups, spreadsheets and unreliable agents. They decided to build a better way.
🛠️
Building
AI meets the travel industry
The team integrated Gemini and Llama AI models to generate detailed, personalized itineraries. Early users were amazed — a complete 7-day itinerary with budget breakdown in under 10 seconds.
🚀
Launch
Trivasta goes live
Trivasta launched with 12 partner agencies across India. Within the first month, over 500 trips were planned and 80 bookings were made. The marketplace model was validated.
📈
Growing
Scaling across India
Today Trivasta has 380+ verified partner agencies, 12,000+ trips planned and a growing community of travelers who choose Trivasta for every adventure. The journey has only just begun.
